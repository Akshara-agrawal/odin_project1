Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:

  2. Prepare ricotta mixture (if using):

    • Repeat layers: pasta, ricotta/b√©chamel, meat sauce, mozzarella.
    • Finish with a layer of pasta, remaining meat sauce, mozzarella, and grated parmesan.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover with aluminium foil (loosely to avoid sticking) and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 15-20 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  5. Rest and Serve:
    • Let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting to help it set properly.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Serving Suggestion

Pair it with garlic bread and a green salad for a balanced Italian meal.
